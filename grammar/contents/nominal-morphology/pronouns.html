Interrogative Pronouns

The attributive form is used when immediately preceding a noun or a noun phrase, normally translatable to English as “which” or “what kind of.” Jenko razmek Stalina vesta\v{s]unek? In which city was Stalin born in? Jako razmek Stalina veesta\v{s]unek? In which of these cities was Stalin born in?
