Vowels

Oral Vowels

Iridian has six pairs of corresponding long and short vowels. With the exception of /a aː/ and /u uː/, long vowels are tenser than their short counterparts. In addition standard Iridian also features the high central vowel [ɨ] as an allophone of /ɛ/ and /ɪ/ and the low central [ɐ] as an allophone of /a/, in unstressed positions.
Table 1.1. Vowel inventory of Standard Iridian.
Close ɪ iː ʏ yː (ɨ) u uː
Mid ɛ eː ɔoː
Open (ɐ) a aː
