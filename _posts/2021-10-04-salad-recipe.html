Ingredients:

mixed greens

croutons

dried figs, chopped

emmental or pecorino, thinly sliced

gouda, cubed (optional)

For the vinaigrette:

4 parts olive oil

2 parts balsamic vinegar

1 part dijon mustard

salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Mix everything in a bowl.
