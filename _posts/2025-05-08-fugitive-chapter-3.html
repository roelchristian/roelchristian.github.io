The following is from a draft written in 2015 or thereabouts, inspired, if memory serves me right, by the opening scene of Pasternak's Dr Zhivago.

In the schoolroom at Charnost, winter settled not simply as a season but as a temperament...

Herr Karoly's past, though seldom spoken of in full, was known in fragments...

He returned to Charnost just before the turn of the century...

In the spring, with the coming of the thaw, Karoly's manner turned rigid again...

Even then, there was a kind of muted solemnity to it all...
