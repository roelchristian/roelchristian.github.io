Hi, I’m Roel. Welcome to my homepage! I’m a twenty-something dilettante from Manila who loves everything about languages. I’m an accountant by training and have worked in finance and fintech since graduating. I also write, and this website is home to my (mostly incoherent) ramblings.

I started this site in 2020 as a new place to self-host my blog, which by then had been running for some four or five years. Over time, however, the site grew to include bits and pieces of my other side projects.
